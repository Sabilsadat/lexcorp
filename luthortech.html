REINVENTING THE WORLD OF TOMORROW

Since the early days of LuthorCorp and the eventual transition to LexCorp, the company has made leaps and bounds in the realms of technological advancement.
Following the acquisitions of Star Labs and the Techno Cosmic Research Institute, LexCorp has become the global leader in weapons and genetics.

S.T.A.R LABS

Acquired by LexCorp in 2014, S.T.A.R Labs has made significant progress in the producing clean, renewable energy in several cities across America through the patented Particle Accelerators.
