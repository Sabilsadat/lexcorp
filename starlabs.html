SCIENTIFIC AND TECHNOLOGICAL ADVANCED RESEARCH LABORATORIES

Acquired by LexCorp in 2014, S.T.A.R Labs has made significant progress in the producing clean, renewable energy in several cities across America through the patented Particle Accelerators. The subdivision has also made advancements in meta-human and dark matter research.

MISSION STATEMENT
FROM OUR LEAD SCIENTIST

“Since its inception, S.T.A.R Labs has aimed to make advancements in science and medicine. Despite some setbacks, our mission to advance has yet to falter. We can never learn to fly without crashing a few times.”

Dr. Harrison Wells has spearheaded S.T.A.R Labs since 2000 prior to the LexCorp acquisition, and is the man responsible for the Particle Accelerator project.

S.T.A.R. Labs PARTICLE ACCELERATOR

The S.T.A.R. Labs particle accelerator, nicknamed The Pipeline, is a device created by S.T.A.R. Labs. On January 7, 2014, the particle accelerator was activated by S.T.A.R. Labs to power all of Central City with clean energy, and to further the advancement of the science and medicine.
Since then, similar accelerators have been built in Metropolis and Star City to generate renewable energy in those cities. The particle accelerator technology has also been used for containment of metahuman criminals in prisons such as Iron Heights Penitentiary, Stryker's Island Penitentiary and Arkham Asylum.
