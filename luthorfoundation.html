OUR CHARITABLE WORK

The Luthor Foundation was established by CEO Lex Luthor to utilize LexCorp assets for the betterment of the everyday person. The Foundation’s philanthropic enterprises include Luthor Hospital, Luthor Home for Children, Luthor Foundation for the Arts and LexMet Square Garden.

Learn More

LUTHOR HOSPITAL

Established in 2001, Luthor Hospital is the main tertiary hospital in Metropolis, with branches throughout the city including Chinatown, Downtown, and the Suicide Slums.
Subsidiaries include Luthor Children's Hospital and Luthor Center For Heart Disease.

LUTHOR HOME FOR CHILDREN

Luthor Home for Children was established to provide shelter to orphaned in displaced children across cities like Metropolis and Central City.
The Foundation facilitates a save haven for less-fortunate children and also arranges for their adoption and relocation to loving foster homes.

LUTHOR FOUNDATION FOR THE ARTS

Luthor Foundation for the Arts is an organization which works with preserving artistic history and practices.
This division of the Foundation has worked closely with Metropolis University and Metropolis Museum valiantly in artistic endeavors.
