TECHNO COSMIC RESEARCH INSTITUTE

Active since the 1980s, the T.C.R.I has advanced the scope of robotics and mutagen research to monumental heights. Following the LuthorCorp acquisition in 2003, the T.C.R.I expanded their operations with divisions in America and Japan. The subdivision is responsible for The Mouser Project, which has facilitated pest control worldwide.

MISSION STATEMENT
FROM OUR LEAD SCIENTIST

“We view problems as a body, our aim is to get to the foot of the problem and provide unforeseen solutions. T.C.R.I’s unconventional approach to technological solutions has made it withstanding for decades.”

Dr. Baxter Stockman is the founder of the T.C.R.I, and has worked side-by-side with both Lionel and Lex Luthor since the 1990s.

The Mouser Project

The Mousers were robots originally created by Dr. Baxter Stockman, under the premise of selling them to the government, as a way of fighting New York City's rat problem.
Each Mouser is about 60 cm. tall and can hold up to five rats. The A.I. can be controlled by radio waves as well as by a central computer.
